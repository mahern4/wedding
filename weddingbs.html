Weekend Plans

We're so excited that you are able to attend our wedding! We want the weekend to be enjoyable and relaxing so don't feel like you have to spend all of your time with us. We understand that you want to make the most of your trip out west. With that said, we would love nothing more than to spend quality time with our family and friends. We will be arriving late Thursday afternoon, so if you're around and want to get together give us a call. Friday night the grooms parents, Tom and Diane Ahern, will be hosting a get together at the Orange Tree Inn for our wedding guests. Saturday after the reception, if you are interested, we were hoping to continue hanging out. Perhaps hit up some nearby wine cellars for tastings! Sunday we are up for anything. For those of you sticking around we could explore the town more, find a trail for hiking or biking, or just lounge around. Monday we head home from a wonderful weekend. Please check back periodically for further updates.
